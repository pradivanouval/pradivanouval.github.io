About me


A piping engineering graduate interested in the oil and gas, power plant, and petrochemical industries. His interest in it makes him always willing to learn something new. Adaptable, creative, hardworking, self-motivated, and able to work under pressure. Also interested in working in a multicultural environment.

Have internship experiences related to his major. During his internship, He has been involved in 2 projects. The first is the Piping and Instrumentation GAN Compressor Project, which works under the Mechanical and Piping Division. Then the second is the Pembangunan of the Aviation Fuel Hydrant System and DPPU Kulon Progo Project under Piping Discipline and Material Control Discipline.

His work experience was spent both in the office and in the field as an intern. Familiar with using software and applying codes and standards related to piping. With his internship experience, He is used to working both in the office and in the field and interacting with many people.
